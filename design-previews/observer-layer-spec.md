# Observer layer placeholder

Observer is the third global meta layer.

Unlock route:
- Universe I-X are the second global meta layer.
- The player can enter the Observer layer only after reaching and completing Universe X.
- The current placeholder also requires 10000 Metaverse Points.

Core rule:
- After entering Observer mode, the normal Progress Knight tabs are hidden.
- Only the Observer tab remains visible.

Main gameplay idea:
- The player no longer directly plays the knight.
- The player manages subordinates, currently named Simulacrums.
- Each Simulacrum starts from zero: Universe I, no Evil, no metaverse progress.
- Simulacrums automatically play Progress Knight.
- Their long-term goal is also to reach Universe X.
- Their progress generates Observer Points.
- Observer Points are spent on global buffs for all Simulacrums and eventually on talent/rank improvements.

Rank plan:
- Trash: free first subordinate, frequent mistakes, weak routing, debuffs.
- Common: stable but slow.
- Skilled: fewer mistakes and better priorities.
- Rare: strong routing and useful bonuses.
- Epic: adapts to universe distortions.
- Legendary: near-optimal route with strong bonuses.

Current implementation:
- This file documents the intended layer.
- The live game currently contains only a safe placeholder.
- Full bot simulation is intentionally deferred until the Universe I-X layer is stable.
